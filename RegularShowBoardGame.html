    • Regular Show Board Game!

    The objective of this board game is for you to race to the finish. However, in order to get there you need to complete tasks that are scattered around your corner of the board. The big point of the game is that if someone gets a powerup and summons the boss, all players need to drop everything and work together to defeat the boss.

    NOTE: This was a team project. I worked on the player cards.
